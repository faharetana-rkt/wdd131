Meet the F-22 Raptor

The F-22 Raptor is a fifth-generation, stealth air superiority fighter developed by Lockheed Martin for the United States Air Force. It was designed to dominate enemy airspace with unmatched speed, maneuverability, and stealth, and also has strong ground-attack, electronic warfare, and signal intelligence capabilities.

Beautiful isn't it? But it's also deadly.

  • Top speed: Mach 2.25 (2,414 km/h / 1,500 mph)
  • Engines: 2 × Pratt & Whitney F119-PW-100 turbofans with thrust vectoring
  • Range: 1,850 miles (2,960 km) with external tanks; 800 miles combat radius
  • Weapons(internal): 6x AIM-120 AMRAAM(Advanced Medium-Range Air-to-Air Missile)
